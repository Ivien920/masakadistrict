Overview of Masaka District

Masaka District is located in the Central Region of Uganda within the Buganda Kingdom. The district lies approximately 179 km from Kampala and is one of the major administrative and commercial centers of the greater Masaka sub-region.

The district is predominantly agricultural and is known for banana production, coffee farming, livestock keeping and trade.

Location & Boundaries

  • Region: Central Uganda
  • Kingdom: Buganda
  • Distance from Kampala: Approximately 179 km
  • Average Elevation: 1,150 metres above sea level

Masaka District lies within Buganda Kingdom and is predominantly occupied by the Baganda. The district hosts cultural sites, clan heritage areas and historical locations linked to Buganda.
